What this tool can't tell you

It can't tell whether you wrote something or an AI did, and it can't tell whether you've cited AI assistance correctly. Those are judgment calls only you (and your course policy) can make. Use the signals below as prompts for reflection, not a pass/fail grade.

What it actually checks for

Warning signals

  • No first-person language ("I think", "in my view") anywhere in the text
  • Very uniform sentence length, with little variation
  • Several generic transition phrases stacked close together

Healthy signals

  • A mix of short and long sentences
  • Specific, concrete details rather than vague generalities
  • Some personal framing or a clear point of view
